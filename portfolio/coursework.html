Course Reflection

Before entering this course, I had taken precalc at my high school, so I knew about functions, polynomials, factoring, as well as some basic trig. This course introduced a lot of new concepts to me, including limits, derivatives, and antiderivatives. I thoroughly enjoyed the course, but was unfortunately unable to get into Calculus II because I didn't register in time. I plan to take the second course in Spring Quarter 2024.
