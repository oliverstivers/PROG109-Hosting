Personal interest

This is a page about one of my personal interests. One of my personal intersts is solving Rubik's cubes. I know how to solve a 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, 5x5, as well as a pyraminx and a square one. My personal best average of 5 3x3 solves is around 15 seconds, with my personal best single 3x3 solve at 8.66 seconds. I have competed in two competitions a couple years ago, and now I just solve for fun.
